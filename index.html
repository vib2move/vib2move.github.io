Applications

The VIB2MOVE system can be applied to various domains including:

  • Healthcare: Monitoring patient movement patterns for rehabilitation.
  • Security: Biometric identification for access control systems.
  • Sports Science: Analyzing athlete performance and movement efficiency.
  • Human-Computer Interaction: Gesture recognition for intuitive interfaces.
